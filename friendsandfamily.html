ACTS OF KINDNESS FOR FRIENDS AND FAMILY

Spread the love with these awesome acts of kindness!

HOME

Jar

Make a jar to appreciate a friend or family member. Fill it with their special qualities that makes them unique.

Chores

Help out your parents with chores unexpectedly. This way they don't have to stress about house chores when they get back from work.

30 minutes

Spend at least 30 minutes a day with your family. Take sometime off to comunicate with your family it can relieve stress and create a stronger bond.

Call

Call a friend rather than texting them. The use of technology has taken over our lives and even easy tasks like speaking to one another.Take some to have a real conversation with a friend.

Appreciation

Tell your parents how much you appreciate them. Your parents are special in many ways they influence you to become the person you are now. Would'nt it be nice to express your gratitude towards them?

Lunch

Pack lunch for your siblings. Siblings bring out the best of you they are there to make you laugh and support you when ever you feel sad. Pack them something nice for lunch, make them their favorite food to show them how much you love them.

Movie/Game night

Arrange a movie/family game night with your family. Share a favorite movie of yours with your family, make some popcorn and chillax. Bond over a movie and share a bunch of laughs!

Homework Help

Help a friend out with homework. If you see a friend struggling in a class offer to help them with their homeowrk. Your friend will really appreciate your concern.

Hiking with Friends

Go on a hike with your friends. Explore the nature around you with some friends. Get some fresh air and exercise and at the same time share some time with your friends.

Help around

Lend a hand to your Grandparents. Grandparents always have our back and we should also have their back. Offer them help with whatever they need they will really appreciate it.

Baby-sit

Offer to baby-sit for a relative for free. Do you have any baby cousins who you would love to spend time with? You should try baby-sitting them. How fun would it be to spend time with a small and adorable baby!

Welcome

Welcome new neighbors around your neighborhood. Moving to a new home can be really hard, if you notice a new neighbor in your neighbprhood say hi and welcome them they will feel so much better
