DIY PROJECTS

Spread the love with these awesome DIYs!

HOME

Hold the Door Open for Anyone

Such a simple act of kindness such as holding the door can lighten someone's day because of an inconvience that they may have.

Smile

Smile to people as they walk by because you never know what kind of day they're having and just a simple smile can quickly change their mood.

Converse with a Stranger

Strike up a conversation with new faces and find out what their personalities and interests are like. Experience the lives of others and spread kindness through the conversations.

Leave Flowers on a Stranger's Doorstep

Go to a stranger's house and leave a flower on their doorstep to suprise them when they step outside.

Pay for Someone's Food

Pay for the stranger who's standing in line behind you or pick out someone who needs help paying for their own cup of coffee or meal. Make their day and hopefully, this small act of kindness will be passed on to others.

Shovel Snow for Your Neighbors

Voluntarily go outside and into your neighbor's front yard and help shovel off the snow that are blocking their drivewway. Pay a small price of effort as a big convenience for others and don't forget to stay warm!

Leave Inspiring Notes

Write short inspiring and hopeful notes on Post-It notes and post it in random places for people to see so that they could be reminded of how amazing they are and will pass on the mindset to everyone around them.

Give Up Your Seat for Someone on the Metro/bus

Give up your seat for elders or people who need it more than you do, or just give up your seat in general for others. Try standing up for a change!

Give Out Water On a Hot Day

Go to places where water is not easily found or accessed or on a hot, burning day and randomly give out cold water to those who needs to cool off.
