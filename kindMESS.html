About kindMESS

Helping you to help others



It comes as no surprise when anyone mentions a tragedy, whether it's about a schools shooting, or the loss of an innocent life at the hands of policemen. We were inspired by Chaos Theory, the idea that one small act can move a mountain, to spread kindness. On kindMESS, we want to encourage everyone to get their hands dirty, make a kind-mess and spread kindness like its Nutella. THE WORLD IS YOUR TOAST.


About Us






Welcome to kindMESS, a non-profit organization that formed out of frustration with the immobility of society. The three of us collectively want to change our passive society to fight fire with water, not fire. Prompted by our love of crafts, activism, and inability to do anything else, we combined our powers to curate this website to make more waves together.
