Spread the love online with these kind acts!

HOME

Follow Motivational & Positive Accounts

You always come first. With social media being over judgemental and very hard to follow these days, make sure that you still have a good environment for yourself. With that said, follow positive accounts that leave no negative impact on you and make you feel better about yourself.

Pledge to Be Kind to People Online

It doesn't matter if it's online or in real life, everyone should be nice to each other regardless of where and when. Pledge to be kind, and be forgiving of others, even if they are the one making hasty comments, for example.

Send Your Friend a Random Kind Message

Now, you are probably aware of this since you communicate with your friends or relatives on social media sites already. But, be sure to remind others of how much you appreciate them or maybe send a random message asking them about how their day is going.

Start the Day with Inspiring News

Some of us aren't really good at waking up early at 6 a.m. and have the spirit of "rise and shine". However, you could be the one to lift up someone's spirit with their morning by posting a positive and inspiring story, hoping to wake them up.

Send a Thank You Email to a Non-profit Organization

Non-profit organizations work hard to maintain their work and integrity within their program. So drop by and send them a "thank you" message about the great work that they're doing to keep them feeling encouraged about the things that they're doing.

Befriend Random Strangers in Need of Emotional Help

Okay, maybe not COMPLETE strangers (because of creeps on the Internet), but mutual friends, and befriend them to talk about the troubles that they may have. Sometimes, you just need a complete, non-biased opinion/insight on the struggles that you're having. Some apps that could help with that is 7 Cups of Tea.

Send Out e-cards for Relatives/friends/colleagues on Special Occasions

Thanksgiving, bithday, Christmas, Halloween, Pie Day, it doesn't really matter what, but as long as you carry out these e-cards, that is all that matters. If you have families and relatives far away, this is your easy way to get a chance to send them a card and celebrate those memorial occasions together.

Volunteer For an Online Organization

If you search up organizations that are hosted online, you'd be suprised at how many things are going on at once. You can start off with something easy such as DoSomething organization, and sign a petition to end a horrible doings.

Reconnect with an Old Friend Online

The online is basically place where everyone can connect automatically, but why not use this to your advantage and stalk (find) your old friends from elementary school and reconnect? Try to see how they're doing by sending a nice message and see where that takes you. :)
