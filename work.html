Spread the love to your co-workers with these kind acts!

Write Your Boss a Thank-you Letter

It's always nice to thank the people who pay you to sit in a cubicle all day. Just kidding, so seriously. Grab a chance and write a kind letter or a card thanking them for giving you a chance to be a part of a great team. Who knows, maybe you'll get that promotion next month. ;)

Leave money in a Vending Machine

Leave a nice little suprise for the next random person using the machine and suprise them. :)

Bring Goodies For Your Colleagues

Bring tiny or big goodies for your co-workers, to celebrate small moments or big events. No matter the occasion, capture these moments with your colleagues and just plan something nice for everyone.

Invite a Co-worker for coffee

Take out a random colleague to grab a quick cup of coffee in the morning and no, this isn't a date (but it could be ;). It's just a small act of kindness that could lead to potentially having a lifelong friend by your side.

Bring Flowers to the Workplace

Try to brighten up your office by placing flowers on your desks or bring some flowers and give it out to your co-workers to brighten up their day too!

Volunteer for a Task

Whether it's a coffee errand for the whole office or volunteering for a project, be the brave one to carry out these tasks for your office and have your face be remembered as the kind one by your colleagues.

Express How Grateful You Are For Your Colleagues

This is an easy one. Even though it's not Thanksgiving, take your pick and it could be a small gesture, or a grand one. Tell them how much you really apprecite their presence and to keep up the good work that they're doing!

Clean Up the Workplace

This one is common sense -clean up after yourself! Not only should you already be doing that, you can lend a hand to the janitor and clean up other mess that isn't yours. It's always a nice thing to help out, even though it can start with a small gesture such as picking up a piece of paper on the floor.

Decorate/Make the Environment Happier in Your Workplace

Give some effort into the place that you spend at least half of your day at. It doesn't have to be from Target, look into cheap DIY's or stuff collected from your home to refine your office for a much more positive feel.
